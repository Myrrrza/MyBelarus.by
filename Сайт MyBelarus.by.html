Memorial complex "Mound of Glory"

The Mound of Glory in Belarus — is a memorial complex in the Smolevichi district of the Minsk region.
The 35-meter monument, installed on top of a bulk hill, was erected in honor of the Great Victory of the
Soviet people over fascism and in memory of the unprecedented feat of Russian soldiers and officers.
The Mound of Glory is located on the 21st km of the Minsk - Minsk National Airport highway
and is a popular attraction of the Belarusian capital, actively visited not only by tourist groups,
but also by foreign government delegations.
